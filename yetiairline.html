About Yeti Airlines

Yeti Airlines is one of the most prominent domestic airlines in Nepal, providing reliable and comfortable air travel to various destinations within the country. Established in 1998, the airline has built a strong reputation for its commitment to safety, customer service, and punctuality.

Key Features:

  • Safety and Comfort: Yeti Airlines prioritizes passenger safety and comfort, with modern aircraft and well-trained staff.
  • Wide Network: Serves a comprehensive network of domestic destinations including Kathmandu, Pokhara, and remote areas.
  • Excellent Service: Known for its friendly and professional service, making every flight a pleasant experience.
  • On-Time Performance: Maintains a strong record of on-time departures and arrivals.

Fleet Information:

The airline operates a fleet of state-of-the-art aircraft, including turboprops and regional jets, tailored for the diverse needs of domestic travel.

Environmental Initiatives:

Yeti Airlines is committed to reducing its environmental footprint through various green initiatives, including fuel-efficient aircraft and waste reduction programs.
